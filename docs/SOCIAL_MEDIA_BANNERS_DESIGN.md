- Dark background (consistent with portfolio: #0a0a0a or #1a1a1a)

- Code/terminal aesthetic mixed with professional polish
- Split design: Open Source Projects (left) + Professional Services (right)

**Typography:**
- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Satoshi, or SF Pro Display)
- **Code/Tech**: Monospace font (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Open Sans)
