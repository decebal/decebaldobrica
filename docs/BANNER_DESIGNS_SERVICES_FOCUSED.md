1. Go to **canva.com** and sign up

   - No watermark
   - Brand kit
   - Better export quality

3. **Set up Brand Colors**:
   - Go to Brand Kit
   - Add colors:
     - `#0a0a0a` (Dark Background)
     - `#14b8a6` (Teal Accent)
